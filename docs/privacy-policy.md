# Privacy Policy — Routine Tracker

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

Routine Tracker is a class-planning tool for yoga and Pilates instructors. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

**Short version: The app collects nothing. All your data stays on your device.**

---

## Data Collection

Routine Tracker does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app:

- Does not require an account or login
- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not contain advertising
- Does not share data with third parties

---

## Data Storage

All routines, class plans, and preferences you create are stored **locally on your device** using your iPhone's built-in storage (UserDefaults/localStorage). This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to any server we control
- Is removed when you delete the app

Your data may be included in your personal iCloud or iTunes backup if you have those enabled on your device — this is controlled entirely by your own Apple account settings.

---

## Permissions

Routine Tracker does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, health data, or any other device permissions.

---

## Children's Privacy

Routine Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Given that the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
